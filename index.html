About us

Our team consists of a varied group of individuals with different background and skillset. We've got a mix of young talent, and experienced IT professionals, coming together to inspire and learn from each other. In 2020 the Norwegian Intelligence Service hosted a CTF to attract cyber talents, and this group was formed following that event for those of us who wanted to continue solving challenges.
